Personal AI & Robotics Research Log

I am exploring human-led intelligent robotics — connecting AI, vision, interaction, and embodied systems.

I use multimodal AI and efficient VLM research as an entry point toward future intelligent robotic systems. My long-term interest lies in robots that can understand the world, interpret human intention, and act as an extension of human capability.

Current focus

Efficient Egocentric VQA

Building research evidence around efficient multimodal AI, evidence selection, latency, token usage, API calls, and future robotic perception.

Direction Reminder

I am not doing AI to move away from robotics. I am doing AI because I want future robots to have a brain that can understand the world, understand humans, and support meaningful action.

Why this website exists

This site is not only a static portfolio. It is a living personal research log. I use it to record small daily progress, questions, experiments, failures, and reflections.

For structured project summaries, please visit the Projects page. This homepage mainly records my ongoing daily research process.
