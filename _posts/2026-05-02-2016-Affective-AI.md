## What I learned
### The process to train a BIG MODEL like GPT. (from Gemini)
1. pre-training. (this process use significant powers)
   
   INPUT: tokens (trillion）
   
   |
   
   Train: model learn the laws, logic, theory....to be a Prediction machine.
   
   |
   
   OUTPUT: Next token prediction ability for model.

2.Supervised fine-tuning: SFT  

  INPUT: trainer have thousands " Questions - Answers" for different tasks.  
  
  |  
  
  Train: learn how to response in correct way.  
  
  |  
  
  OUTPUT: the model be a draft assistant.

3.Reforcement learning or RFHL  

   INPUT: human review and rank the different answers from Model. Base on those, train a 'judger model' to mark the answers from Model.  
   
   |  
   
   TRAIN: LLM generate answers, the 'judger model' mark those answers. 
   <img width="309" height="58" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/466d1792-4bc0-4e96-a779-a40afd30fb99" />  
   if one answer gets high mark, the parameters in LLM will be changed to increase this kind of answer's probability.  
   
   |  
   
   Tips: The interesting 'goblin' must be from here, 'goblin's weight was changed too high in the marking process of reward model.  ---> reward drift. 
         then cuz "reward hacking": model was cheating by using highly weighted worlds.
