About

Xi Nan

I am currently studying MSc Robotics & Artificial Intelligence at University College London, with a background in Electrical & Electronic Engineering.

Direction

I am exploring human-led intelligent robotics: systems where AI, vision, interaction, and embodied components help robots understand the world and support meaningful human action.

I do not see AI as separate from robotics. I see it as one part of the future robotic brain: a layer that can help robots interpret scenes, understand intention, and act as an extension of human capability.

Research Interests

Embodied AI Vision-Language-Action World Models Robot Learning Human-led Robotics Shared Autonomy Robotic Systems Integration Efficient Multimodal AI Affective AI

I am also interested in exploring Affective AI — how AI can simulate and understand human emotions for mental health support and psychological analysis. This complements my robotics research by helping robots better understand human emotional states and respond appropriately.
